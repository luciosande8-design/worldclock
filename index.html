WorldClock.live
Global Time & Productivity Hub

WorldClock.live is a free real-time world clock covering 120+ cities across 80+ countries and 38 time zones. Built and maintained independently from Beira, Mozambique.

Our mission is to make time accessible to everyone — from professionals coordinating across time zones to students managing their focus with the built-in Pomodoro system.

The platform supports 10 languages with automatic detection, works as an installable app (PWA), and is designed with a strong focus on African cities and communities.
